Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required?

You need your resume, academic transcripts, ID proof, and relevant certificates. Ensure all documents are up to date.

How to prepare for technical interviews?

Focus on core concepts, practice coding problems, and review your projects. Use the resources provided here for guidance.

What is the typical placement process?

The process usually includes resume screening, aptitude tests, technical rounds, and HR interviews. It can vary by company.
